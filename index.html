I`m a Spanish artisan, designer and architect based in Italy. My work is based on the application of craftmanship in objects and spaces design with a contemporary approach

Influenced by the Mediterranean traditions and culture of my hometown - Valencia - my work develops around ceramics and textiles. Each creation is the result of the union between design and craftmanship and emphasizes the beauty of handmade things and their imperfections.
